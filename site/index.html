hello, wanderer! welcome to my corner.
...the corner is still materialising. quite slowly. we can wait together, if youd like.
hm? i shant be waiting? why not?

you want to know about me?
well, i am a non-binary programmer living in new zealand. life is almost alright. time never seems to be steady.

i like playing video games. you can find parts of my memories with them, here and there. some video games have changed my life, and ensure i will forever be linked to them. i dont even play baba is you, not really.

i have a strange friend. you may have seen him on the way down here. his name is nikola tesla. im not sure if he knows that. im not sure if he thinks names are important. he occupies more of my waking moments than any other living entity. i dont think hes consuming my thoughts, but someone must be.

i have walked the halls of css. not very far, but you can see what ive found and learnt here. the contrivances and incongruities are still not quite familiar to me.

i am being intentionally obtuse, sorry. im mostly talking to myself. you seem to be listening, though. whats your name?

oh, youve forgotten it?
thats alright. you neednt remember mine, either. you can call me apia, if you need to.
its quite odd, how people so often expect you to name them -- not even with your own design, but theirs.

the stars are nice, arent they? we watch them and they watch us back. i wonder if they think we are nice, too.
thanks for being here. i appreciate it.
